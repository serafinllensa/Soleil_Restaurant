Our Story

A Passion for
Exceptional
Dining.

Soleil was born from a single conviction: that a meal, when crafted with intention, becomes a memory. Founded in 2019 by Chef Isabelle Moreau, our kitchen draws from the rich traditions of Lyon and Provence, reimagined for the modern palate.

Every dish tells a story of season and place. We source exclusively from trusted local farms, rotating our menu as the land dictates — not the other way around.

Meet the Chef

Isabelle
Moreau

With over 20 years of experience across three-star Michelin kitchens in Paris, Lyon and New York, Chef Isabelle brings an uncompromising dedication to craft. Trained under Alain Ducasse, she opened Soleil in 2019 with a singular vision: classical French technique, elevated by local terroir.
